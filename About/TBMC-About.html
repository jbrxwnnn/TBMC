About Me

Hello everyone - my name is Jake and it is my pleasure to welcome you to The Bearded Man Cave!

I created The Bearded Man Cave back in 2017 when I moved into my flat with my partner. This was the first time that I ever thought about content creation. I started small with a separate Instagram account and began posting gaming related photos every now and then (both video and tabletop).

Flash-forward 2-years and we’re at the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic. During this time I was working loads and needed something more to unwind - this is when I started live streaming. I started out trying to mix up my content, but I ended up settling on pushing towards FIFA (I was playing a lot anyway so might as well carry on!)

Life started to open back up and then streaming became rarer and rarer. Unfortunately, streaming isn’t something I’ve been able to put much effort into mid-2021 onwards, but watch this space for more in the future! Until then, you can jump onto the Clips page to see some of my highlights!

I’m now aspiring to change careers and move into Software/Web Development. This is a BIG change of pace from what I currently do so I am having to divide my focus into pursuing a better future for myself. This process is exactly where this website has come from. This will be one of my first - and hopefully many - projects utilising skills that I have picked up from the courses I am undertaking. Any tips, tricks or feedback will be very welcome and be sure to check out my Blog page and GitHub profile to have a deeper look into what I’m doing!
